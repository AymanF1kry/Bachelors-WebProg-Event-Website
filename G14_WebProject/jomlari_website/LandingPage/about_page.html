About Event

#1 Biggest running event in Malaysia

At Jom Lari 2024, we take pride in being the #1 running event in Malaysia. Our commitment to excellence, scenic routes, and a vibrant atmosphere has made us a household name. Thousands join us annually to experience not just a race, but a celebration of fitness, unity, and shared achievements. We're more than an event; we're a movement inspiring a healthier lifestyle. Join us at Jom Lari, where every step is a journey towards wellness, camaraderie, and unforgettable moments. We also received our award from Dato' Seri Najib Razak for achieving more than 128,888 contestants

Our 2024 Event Poster

We are thrilled to announce our inaugural running event of the year, scheduled for the 28th of April 2024. This event promises to be a celebration of fitness, community, and shared philanthropy as we embark on a journey towards a healthier and more united future. Join us on this auspicious day as we lace up our running shoes for a cause. This year's event holds special significance as it serves as a charity run dedicated to addressing the urgent needs of those facing hunger. All proceeds from this event will be dedicated to supporting charitable initiatives, symbolizing our commitment to giving back to the community that has continually supported us. Your participation will not only contribute to your well-being but will also make a meaningful impact on the lives of those in need. Let's run together for a healthier community and a brighter future.

Venue: KLCC Park
Time: 6:00 A.M
Date: 28th April 2024
