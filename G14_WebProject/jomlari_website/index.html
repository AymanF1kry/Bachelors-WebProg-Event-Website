Welcome to Jom Lari 2024, where passion meets the pavement!

At Jom Lari, we believe in the power of running to transform lives and build communities. With a shared commitment to health, wellness, and the thrill of the run, our annual event has become a celebration of active living.

Our Mission

Inspiring individuals to embrace a healthier lifestyle through the joy of running. Jom Lari is not just a race; it's a journey towards personal wellness, community unity, and achieving new milestones together.

Why Jom Lari?

Inclusive Community

Whether you're a seasoned marathoner or taking your first steps in the running world, Jom Lari welcomes participants of all levels and backgrounds.

Beautiful Courses

Experience the scenic routes carefully curated to showcase the best of our vibrant city and create unforgettable memories.

Wellness Celebration

Beyond the finish line, enjoy wellness activities, music, and a festive atmosphere that celebrates achievements, big and small.
